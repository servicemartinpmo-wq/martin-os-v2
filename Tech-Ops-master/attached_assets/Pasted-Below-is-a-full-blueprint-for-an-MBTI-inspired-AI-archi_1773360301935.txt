Below is a full blueprint for an MBTI-inspired AI architecture with INTJ as the core, but incorporating the strongest reasoning traits across all types. Treat MBTI as a design metaphor for cognitive modules, not a literal neuroscience model. The goal is a modular AI “brain” you can implement for an autonomous tech-support system.
